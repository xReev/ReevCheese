**changelog:** `01/05/2026` 

## Menu / Languages
- **Added Spanish + Russian Menu Support** - You can now use the menu in **Español** or **Русский**.
- **More Text Is Now Translated** - More parts of the menu now follow your selected language (labels, buttons, and common messages).
- **Tooltips Now Translate** - Hover tooltips now show in your chosen language instead of only English.
- **Global Search Translated** - The search window now shows translated text (search prompt, columns, “no results”).
- **Radio Section Translated** - Radio tab text like “Now Playing”, “Loading”, “Search”, “Favorites”, and buttons are now translated.
