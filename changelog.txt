changelog: 01/09/2026 - 07:33 AM [GMT+01:00] 

Menu - User Profile
Discord Avatar - Your Discord profile picture now shows in the menu header as a circular avatar.
Username Display - Your Discord username appears next to your avatar in the top-right of the menu.
Fallback Avatar - If your avatar can't load, a colored circle with your first initial is shown instead.
Animated Avatar Support - Animated Discord profile pictures are now supported (converted to static for performance).

Watermark
Username in Watermark - The watermark now shows "REEV | YourUsername" so you can see who's logged in at a glance.

Notes
You may need to re-login with the loader for your avatar to appear.
Your profile info is securely stored locally and fetched from Discord.
